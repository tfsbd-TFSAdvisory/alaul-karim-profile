About the Company

Trusted & Leading Trade Finance Advisory in Bangladesh

Trade Finance Solution Bangladesh (TFSBD) is a specialized advisory and service provider in international trade finance, delivering secure, scalable, and compliant solutions for cross-border commerce.

Our Mission

To provide trusted, innovative, and client-focused trade finance solutions that simplify global commerce — ensuring that local enterprises can engage with global stakeholders with transparency, efficiency, and absolute regulatory confidence.


Our Vision

To be the leading advisory partner in Bangladesh and APAC for secure, scalable, and ethical trade finance systems — elevating Bangladesh's standing in the international market.

Markets Served

  • Importers & Exporters across Bangladesh
  • Manufacturers & Service Providers
  • SMEs entering international markets
  • Global banking & financial institutions
  • Financial consultancies & advisory firms

Professional Competencies

Strategic Expertise & Technical Mastery

Cross-Border Trade Financing

Expert structuring of Letters of Credit (LCs), guarantees, forfaiting, and supply chain finance. Orchestrates multi-million-dollar transactions between international banks and domestic partners.

Regulatory Compliance

Deep navigation of Bangladesh Bank regulations and global trade licensing frameworks. Engineered bilingual compliance libraries setting new benchmarks for trade adherence in the region.

Fintech Integration & Localization

Contextualizing global platforms such as Taulia (integrated via SAP) for local SME adoption — removing significant barriers to entry for Bangladeshi enterprises engaging international finance.

Business Model Design

Creating resilient strategic frameworks for trade facilitation. Designing modular, client-facing digital resources that maximize efficiency and ensure compliance readiness in multi-jurisdictional transactions.

Executive Communication

High-level reporting, partnership statements, and bilingual advisory for global and local stakeholders. Bridging complex international standards with native-language accessibility.

Global Partnership Building

Connecting local enterprises with international financial networks. Partnerships with 45+ international banks ensuring seamless trade operations across borders and regulatory jurisdictions.

Service Portfolio
